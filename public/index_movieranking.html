My Top Three Movies Of All Time

PULP FICTION

An absolute work of art. its a must watch movie. From the story to the acting it is top notch

INTERSTELLAR

I have watched the movie just so many times and yet it still amazes me. The graphics and the music by Hans Zimmer will always leave me mesmerized.

THE GODFATHER

I have no words to express how beautiful this movies is. It's just the best.
